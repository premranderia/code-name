How To Play
1) Game is played between 2 teams Red & Blue
2) 6+ Players can play split in 2 teams
3) Each team will elect 1 spy master and they both will have access to all cards and its color
4) Spy masters can access a board by retrieving the game by ID below
5) When a game is started, the board is blank with red/blue/yellow card values hidden
6) Spymaster will give 1 word clue that can point to multiple cards
eg: Red teams spy master can say "Food 2", that means there are 2 cards on board that has some relation with food.
7) The game ends when all of one team's agents are identified (winning the game for that team),[3] or when one team has identified the assassin (grey card)
