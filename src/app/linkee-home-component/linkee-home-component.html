How To Play
1) 5-6 Players can play, enter their names below
2) One would be God, Master who knows all options, clues & answer (to view enter the gameID in another device and retrieve the game)
3) Game will start with a question card on screen with value either L   I     N   K   E
4) 4 options are given which has a link.
5) If the player guessed it correct, Master will click on player name to update his score card
Eg: Jack answered the question with card value 'L' right, then his score card will have L1 added to his snack-bar-container
6) First player to have L1   I1   N1   K1   E1, all 1 wins !!
